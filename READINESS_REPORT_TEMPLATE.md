Sovereign Compute Standard (SCS‑1)
Dynamic Artificial Intelligence Laboratories (DAIL)

Purpose of This Report
This template provides a structured format for institutions to document their readiness to implement the Sovereign Compute Standard (SCS‑1). It is intended for internal leadership, auditors, governance authorities, and external partners who require a formal statement of preparedness.
This report is non‑normative and does not replace the requirements of SCS‑1.

1. Executive Summary
Provide a concise overview of the institution’s current readiness posture.
Include:
• 	Overall readiness rating
• 	Major strengths
• 	Key gaps or risks
• 	Summary of next steps

2. Institutional Context
Describe the institution’s environment and mission as it relates to sovereign compute.
Include:
• 	Organizational structure
• 	Critical systems and workloads
• 	Regulatory or compliance obligations
• 	Existing compute infrastructure (classical, accelerated, distributed, edge, quantum‑hybrid)

3. Assessment Methodology

7. Implementation Roadmap

Phase 2 — Intermediate Actions (3–12 months)

• 	Reproducibility pipelines

Phase 3 — Long‑Term Actions (12+ months)
• 	Continuous compliance
• 	Advanced provenance and reproducibility capabilities
• 	Integration with quantum‑hybrid systems (if applicable)
